MT 15

Yamaha MT-15 Version 2.0 is powered by 155 cc engine.This MT-15 Version 2.0 engine generates a power of 18.4 PS @ 10000 rpm and a torque of 14.1 Nm @ 7500 rpm. The claimed mileage of MT-15 Version 2.0 is 47.94 kmpl. Yamaha MT-15 Version 2.0 gets Disc brakes in the front and rear. The kerb weight of MT-15 Version 2.0 is 139 Kg. Yamaha MT-15 Version 2.0 has Tubeless Tyre and Alloy Wheels.
